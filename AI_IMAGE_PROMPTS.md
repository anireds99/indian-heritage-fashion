## General Styling Notes:
- All designs use cream/beige colored garments for vintage aesthetic
- Sepia-toned or vintage photograph style for main imagery
- Bilingual text (English + regional language) at top
- Bold statement text at bottom in gold/bronze with decorative elements
- Subtitle banners with historical context
- Professional studio lighting with soft shadows
- Clean light background (white to light gray)
- Realistic fabric textures showing hoodie details (drawstrings, pockets, ribbing)
- Front-facing product view
- 8K quality, e-commerce ready
- Storytelling approach celebrating Indian heritage

## Tips for Best Results:
1. Use these prompts in AI tools like DALL-E 3, Midjourney v6, or Leonardo.ai
2. For Midjourney, add parameters: `--ar 3:4 --style raw --v 6`
3. Adjust "vintage" to "modern" if you want contemporary photography style
4. Request multiple variations and select the best
5. Upscale final images to at least 4K resolution for print quality
